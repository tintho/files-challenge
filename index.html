Sheik

Sheik is part of a race called the Sheikah, also known as the Shadow Folk. Sheik and the other Sheikah are ninja-like warriors who have sworn to protect the Royal Family of Hyrule. Sheik appears to be a male and has long blonde hair. He is a very sneaky and mysterious character who randomly dissapears and re-appears throughout the game.

Sheik is one of the characters in the game Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time. He is a mysterious character that appears when Link (the hero) is sent to the future. He tries to aid link by teaching him songs of teleportation. Later in the game, Link finds out that Sheik is not a male. Sheik is none other than the hiding Princess Zelda, the princess that he had aquainted himself with seven years prior.
